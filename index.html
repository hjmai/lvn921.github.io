My Story

My name is Lanvi Nguyen and I was born and raised in Southern California. I graduated from University of California, Irvine in December of 2015 and I am currently employed at a wonderful company, Amazon, as a Software Development Engineer. My life might sound amazing now, but I have had many challenges and obstacles along the way.

As a young girl, I struggled with math. I noticed that the top math students in my classes were only boys. When I asked one of them for help, the boy told me, "You wouldn't get it because you're a girl."

Women in Tech {WIT} Scholarship

The {WIT} Scholarship is a $1,000 scholarship intended to support and empower young women who stand by their passion for Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or other related technical discipline.

However, the process will be different than the usual essay submission. Rather, I want to emphasize that art and creativity are important, so to qualify for the sponsorship, every candidate must submit a 8.5 x 11 inch illustration of the scholarship theme in black and white using only a pen or a pencil and a piece of paper.

This year's theme is

"Engineering is an art."

DEADLINE: Must be postmarked by May 30.
